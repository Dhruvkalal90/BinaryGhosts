Courses

Take the first step toward your new career Get professional-level training and earn a credential recognized by leading companies.

01.BCA

BCA is a three year undergraduate degree programme in computer applications. Candidates who want to pursue a career as a computer developer, programmer and join any profession in the information technology field can opt for the BCA degree programme.

02.BBA

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a 3-year undergraduate program and it covers key subjects like marketing, finance, and human resources, with the specialization options in areas such as international business and digital marketing.

03.B Tech

Bachelor of Technology, which is an undergraduate level professional engineering degree course spanning 4 years. B Tech is a field related to computers and technology that includes a detailed study of programming and computer software applications.

About Us

Revolutionizing Campus Life Through Technology EduConnect is a smart campus platform designed to enhance student engagement, faculty efficiency, and administrative workflows through an integrated digital experience. It serves as a one-stop solution for accessing academic resources, managing schedules, tracking progress, and fostering seamless communication within the campus.

Personalized Learning

Students can access course materials, assignments, and announcements in one place.

Faculty Management

A streamlined system for faculty to manage student records, attendance, and evaluations.

Campus Services

Simplified access to hostel booking, fee payments, library, and transport services.

Smart Analytics

AI-powered dashboards provide insights into academic and extracurricular performance.

24/7 Connectivity

A seamless communication hub connecting students, faculty, and administration.

Automated Notifications & Reminders

Stay updated with assignment deadlines, event schedules, and campus alerts.
